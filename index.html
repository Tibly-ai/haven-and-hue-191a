Our Approach

Calm spaces don't happen by accident

Haven & Hue was founded on a simple belief: a well-staged home shouldn't feel staged. It should feel like an exhale — warm, unhurried, and quietly beautiful.

Led by principal designer Mara Ellison, our small team blends real-estate savvy with a designer's eye, so every choice serves both the buyer's heart and your bottom line.

Meet the Team

  1. Walkthrough & Vision

    We tour the property, study its light and layout, and identify what buyers need to feel.

  2. Curate & Style

    Furniture, art, and textures are selected from our inventory and installed in a single day.

  3. List & Celebrate

    Your home hits the market photo-ready. We handle de-staging after the offer is signed.
