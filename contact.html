Before You Write

A few things people often ask

How quickly can you stage my listing?

Most vacant stagings are installed within five to seven business days of our walkthrough — and in a single day once we arrive. Rush timelines are often possible; just tell us your target list date.

Is the consultation really free?

Yes. Your first conversation and initial walkthrough carry no cost and no obligation. You'll leave with honest recommendations either way.

Do you work with realtors?

Constantly — about half our projects come through agent partners. Ask about our realtor program, which includes priority scheduling and pre-listing walkthroughs.

What does staging typically cost?

Occupied stagings often begin around $450; full vacant stagings vary with square footage and typically range from $2,200 to $5,500 for a 60-day term. Every quote is itemized and fixed — no surprises.
